Roof of Africa Overview

The Roof of Africa is a premier, historic (since 1967) hard eduro motorcycle rally held annually in Lesotho's Maloti Mountains. Known as the Mother of Hard Enduro, it tests riders across 250-350km of brutal, high-altitude terrain. The 3-day event features Gold, Silver, Bronze, and Iron classes, often acting as a FIM Hard Enduro Wold Championship finale.
